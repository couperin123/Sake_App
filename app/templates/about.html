Motivation

Japanese Sake (rice wine) is gaining popularity in United States. In fact, United States is the largest importer of Japanese Sake. Sake is often considered to be mysterious to many customers in western countries, and therefore it makes finding the "right" Sake to drink not trivial. SakeOrb is a Sake recommender app that helps people find their favorite Sake.

Using SakeOrb

For starters, SakeOrb provides popular items in each Sake category. SakeOrb is also capable of recommending similar Sakes to the user's choice by machine learning algorithm.
